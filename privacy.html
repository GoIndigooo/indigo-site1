Privacy Policy

Last updated April 2026. Plain language. No legalese.

Who We Are

GoIndigo is an autonomous Google Ads management service. We connect to your Google Ads account via the Google Ads API and manage campaigns on your behalf.

What We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, business info from our contact form), performance data from your Google Ads account (via API), and analytics data (GA4, Search Console) when you authorize access.

How We Use It

We use your data exclusively to provide the service: building and managing your campaigns, generating performance reports, and communicating with you about your account.

Google API Services -- User Data Policy

GoIndigo's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

What Google Data We Access

  • Google Ads API: Campaign performance, keyword data, bid settings, budget information, ad copy, conversion data, and impression share data.
  • Google Analytics (GA4) API: Website session data, conversion events, bounce rate, and traffic source data.
  • Google Search Console API: Organic search impressions, clicks, and keyword ranking data.

Restrictions on Google Data Use

  • We do not sell Google API data to any third party.
  • Data from one client's account is never used to inform decisions for another client's account.
  • Google user data is not used to train AI models.
  • No changes are made to your account without your explicit approval.

Revoking Access

You may revoke GoIndigo's access at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions. Stored OAuth tokens are deleted within 30 days of cancellation.

Data Security

OAuth refresh tokens are stored using industry-standard encryption. Access tokens are short-lived and refreshed only as needed.

CCPA / GDPR
