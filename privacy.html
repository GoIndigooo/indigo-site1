2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you submit our contact form, we collect the following information:

  • First and last name

  • Service type and target market

When you use the Client Portal, we collect your login credentials (email and password) which are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not transmit or store portal login data on any external server.

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages and does not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or behavioral analytics tools. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking technologies on this site.

We use the information you provide solely to:

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Contact form data is transmitted via Formspree's secure infrastructure and delivered to our email inbox. We do not operate our own database or server for storing contact submissions.

Client portal data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of any data you submit.

6. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email inbox for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and manage any resulting business relationship. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Portal data stored in localStorage is retained until you clear your browser data or until it is manually removed.
